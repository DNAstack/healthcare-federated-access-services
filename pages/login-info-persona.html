The Persona Playground allows real users to act as a Testing Persona.

This Playground Edition of the platform is only for use with synthetic and public datasets.

Login as a testing persona by choosing one of the persona options from the list along the left. Permissions to resources will vary by persona. Typically the "Administrator" persona will have permission to modify system configurations, including adding or modifying other personas.

Please do not modify Administrator personas, and only modify configurations on your own "realm".
